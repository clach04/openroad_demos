Laurel and Hardy, with their faithful mule Dinah, head for Brushwood Gulch to deliver the deed to a gold mine to Mary Roberts, the orphan daughter of their dead partner. Will Mickey Finn and his wife, Lola, a blonde showgirl, succeed in their scheme to steal the deed from its rightful owner?  "We handed the deed to the wrong person, " Laurel exclaims.  "That's the first mistake we made since that guy sold us the Brooklyn Bridge."
