Suspense, romance, and comedy all in one. An innocent Canadian in London attempts to save a woman's life, only to find out that she is a counter-agent on the trail of a master criminal who is trying to smuggle military secrets out of the country. When she is murdered, he becomes the prime suspect. Fleeing through the Scottish highlands, he ends up handcuffed to a beautiful woman who refuses to believe his story. Do you believe it? And what are the 39 steps?
